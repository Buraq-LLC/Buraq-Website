Why Buraq?

Buraq AI was created to protect civilians and uphold human rights in the world’s most challenging environments. This includes environments of asymmetric warfare, where traditional surveillance methods fall short. Our mission is to empower humanitarian operations and international justice with advanced, transparent, and ethical intelligence technology.

  • Purpose-built for humanitarian and civilian protection
  • Forensic integrity and accountability at every step
  • AI-driven insights for rapid, ethical decision-making
  • Technology to resist censorship, authoritarianism, and global imperialism.

Tactical Autonomy

Buraq empowers operators with a unified ISR interface—streamlining intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance across conflict zones. Every action is logged with forensic integrity, supporting accountability and global transparancy. Our platform fuses edge AI, real-time data, and resilient architecture to deliver actionable insights, reduce decision latency, and safeguard mission outcomes.

Real-Time Monitoring

Deploy drones and AI to monitor operational areas and detect threats in real-time, even when network connectivity is compromised.

  • Live AO monitoring with dynamic map overlays
  • Automated zone alerts and threat escalation
  • Secure data synchronization between edge units

Edge AI Detection

Deploy autonomous vision at the edge to detect threats even when the network goes dark. Our edge AI capabilities ensure continuous operation in disconnected environments.

  • Containerized AI microservices for real-time inference
  • MQTT-based streaming for sub-100ms event propagation
  • Federated learning with privacy-preserving updates

Forensic Evidence Chain

Cryptographically hash-chained mission logs record all ISR events with region and severity metadata, ensuring tamper-evident auditability for international justice.

  • Geo-tagged captures with tamper-evident logs
  • Time-stamped, ICC-compliant dossiers
  • Automated evidence export in standardized formats

Signal Intelligence

Multilingual NLP scans chatter, reports, and sensor feeds to correlate drone and sensor data, predicting threats before they materialize.

  • Entity recognition and sentiment analysis
  • Bayesian correlation of multimodal data
  • Early-warning models with rapid escalation
