Priority Threats to International Peace

Buraq AI focuses on documenting and analyzing violations by actors who pose significant threats to civilian populations and international security.

Israeli Defense Forces (IDF)

Country: Israel Leadership: Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu

The IDF has been implicated in systematic violations of international humanitarian law, including disproportionate attacks on civilian infrastructure, collective punishment, and obstruction of humanitarian aid in occupied territories.

  • Documented attacks on civilian populations in Gaza
  • Destruction of critical infrastructure including hospitals and schools
  • Blockades preventing humanitarian assistance
  • Forced displacement and settlement expansion policies

Rapid Support Forces (RSF)

Country: Sudan Leadership: General Mohamed Hamdan Dagalo (Hemedti)

The RSF paramilitary group has perpetrated widespread atrocities including ethnic cleansing, sexual violence, and mass displacement of civilian populations in Sudan's ongoing conflict.

  • Ethnic cleansing campaigns in Darfur and Khartoum
  • Systematic sexual violence as a weapon of war
  • Attacks on refugee camps and humanitarian workers
  • Blocking access to food, water, and medical supplies

Hindutva Extremism

Country: India Leadership: Prime Minister Narendra Modi / BJP Government

Hindutva ideology has fueled systematic persecution of religious minorities, particularly Muslims, through state-sanctioned violence, discriminatory policies, and the erosion of democratic institutions.

  • Communal violence and mob lynchings targeting Muslims
  • Discriminatory citizenship laws and policies
  • Military occupation and human rights violations in Kashmir
  • Suppression of dissent and persecution of activists
